About Jenny & David – Professional Music Duo from London

Jenny & David are a London-based acoustic duo offering high-quality live music for weddings, private events, corporate functions, and pub gigs. Since relocating to the capital, they’ve built a strong reputation for their warm, polished sound and engaging performances across a wide range of venues and event types.

From laid-back ballads to lively uptempo sets, their repertoire spans from 1950s classics to today’s hits — making them a versatile act suitable for wedding receptions, garden parties, corporate drinks receptions, and live pub entertainment.

Their journey has taken them from street performances to iconic venues, including Winter Wonderland (2016–2018) and various Pizza Express Live locations. Whether playing for a wedding crowd, a birthday party, a corporate dinner, or a Friday night pub audience, they adapt seamlessly to every setting, creating the perfect musical atmosphere.

In 2019, they joined the Elite Artist Agency, further establishing their presence in London’s live music scene. Since then, they’ve performed across Soho, Greenwich, Bexley, Chiswick, Colchester, and beyond — trusted by event organisers, pub managers, and private clients for their reliability, professionalism, and ability to connect with any audience.

With a refined repertoire, top-tier vocals, and a natural musical chemistry, Jenny & David continue to make every event personal, memorable, and musically outstanding — whether it's a wedding ceremony, a corporate celebration, or a live acoustic evening at the pub.
