About Jenny & David – Professional Music Duo from London

Jenny & David are a professional acoustic duo based in London, available for weddings, private parties, corporate events, and live pub gigs across London and Greater London. Their live acoustic music is known for its warm, elegant sound and their ability to create the perfect mood for any occasion.

Specialising in wedding entertainment, private event music, and corporate acoustic sets, Jenny & David deliver high-quality live music with a personal touch. Their repertoire includes everything from romantic ballads to lively uptempo covers — perfect for ceremonies, drinks receptions, garden parties, and intimate venues.

This London acoustic duo is known for creating the sound and energy of a full band using a professional loop pedal setup, layering guitar, vocals, harmonies, and rhythm live on stage. There are no backing tracks — everything is performed live, creating an authentic and engaging show that brings every event to life.

They’ve played in iconic locations such as Winter Wonderland (2016–2018) and multiple Pizza Express Live venues. Whether you're planning a wedding in Greater London, a private function in the suburbs, or a live acoustic set in a pub, Jenny & David bring experience, charm, and outstanding musicality.

Since joining the Elite Artist Agency in 2019, they’ve performed throughout London boroughs including Soho, Greenwich, Chiswick, Bexley, and as far as Colchester. Their professionalism and ability to connect with all types of audiences make them a top choice for anyone seeking a live acoustic duo for hire in London.

If you’re looking to book a wedding duo in London, organise a private party with live music, or need a reliable acoustic act for your pub or corporate event, Jenny & David will tailor the experience to suit your event. Their live sets are always heartfelt, professional, and perfectly adapted to your audience.
