About Jenny & David - Biography and Upcoming Performances

Jenny & David are a London-based acoustic duo offering high-quality live music for weddings, private events, and corporate functions. Since relocating to the capital, they’ve built a strong reputation for their warm, polished sound and engaging performances.

Their journey has taken them from street performances to iconic venues, including Winter Wonderland (2016–2018) and various Pizza Express locations. Whether playing to a wedding crowd, a garden party, or a relaxed dinner setting, they adapt seamlessly to every environment, creating the perfect atmosphere.

In 2019, they joined the Elite Artist Agency, further establishing their presence in London’s live music scene. Since then, they’ve performed across Soho, Greenwich, Bexley, Chiswick, Colchester and beyond — trusted by event organisers for their reliability, professionalism, and ability to connect with any audience.

With a refined repertoire and a natural musical chemistry, Jenny & David continue to make every event personal, memorable, and musically outstanding.

Since their relocation to London, they've captivated audiences across the city, from busking on the streets to performing at iconic venues. You may have spotted them performing at Winter Wonderland in 2016, 2017, and 2018, or enjoying a pizza while serenading guests at Pizza Express venues. They've brought their music to countless weddings, garden parties, and events, creating unforgettable experiences wherever they go.

In January 2019, Jenny & David joined the Elite Artist Agency, and since then, they’ve graced a variety of venues across London and Greater London—from the vibrant streets of Soho to the charming corners of Greenwich, Bexley, Colchester, and Chiswick, as well as every corner of the city, bringing their music to every neighborhood, event, and occasion.

Their infectious energy, undeniable talent, and unwavering commitment to their craft make them a standout act on the live music scene.
